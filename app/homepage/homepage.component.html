Welcome To We school for applied Technology

WE Schools for Applied Technology are a collaboration between Telecom Egypt and the Egyptian Ministry of Education and Technical Education. The schools offer three-year technical secondary education programs in the fields of information and communications technology, computer technology, electronics systems, and electrical systems.

The schools are designed to bridge the gap between education and the labor market, and to provide students with the skills they need to succeed in high-demand fields. The curriculum is based on international standards, and the schools offer state-of-the-art facilities and equipment.
